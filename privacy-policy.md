This Privacy Policy explains how **minsu.digital** (“the Site”) handles information when you visit **minsu.digital**.

## Overview

This website is a **static, content-only blog**. It does not offer user accounts, comments, forms, or other mechanisms for submitting personal information. All content is published by the site owner and includes technology advice, tutorials, comics, and personal opinions.

The Site is intentionally designed to collect as little information as possible.

## Information We Do Not Collect

The Site does **not** collect or store:

- Names, email addresses, or contact information  
- User accounts or login credentials  
- Comments, messages, or submissions  
- Uploaded files or media  
- Payment or billing information  

You may browse the Site without providing any personal data.

## Hosting and Network Information

This Site is hosted using **GitHub Pages**, a static website hosting service operated by GitHub (a Microsoft subsidiary).

The Site itself does **not** collect, log, or analyze visitor network or device information. However, GitHub Pages and its underlying infrastructure may automatically process limited technical data as part of normal website delivery and security operations. This may include:

- IP address  
- Browser or device information  
- Request timestamps  
- Requested URLs  

This information is processed by GitHub/Microsoft and is **not accessible to or controlled by the Site owner**. It is used for purposes such as site delivery, reliability, security, and abuse prevention.

For more information, please refer to GitHub’s privacy policy.

## Cookies

The Site does **not** use cookies for advertising, analytics, or user tracking.

Any cookies that may be present are limited to those required by the hosting platform or browser for basic functionality. No third-party marketing or profiling cookies are used.

## Third-Party Services

The Site does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising networks, or marketing tools.

Aside from the hosting provider required to serve the Site, no third parties are intentionally provided access to visitor data.

## External Links

The Site may contain links to external websites. The Site owner is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites. Visitors should review the privacy policies of any third-party sites they choose to visit.

## Content Disclaimer

All content on the Site is provided for informational and entertainment purposes only and reflects the personal opinions and experiences of the author. Nothing on the Site should be considered professional, legal, security, or financial advice.

## Data Security

Because the Site does not collect personal data, there is no user information stored or sold by the Site owner. Reasonable measures are taken to ensure the Site remains secure and available.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes to the Site or applicable regulations. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
